Overview

Do You Know Yourself?

Knowing yourself is one of the most challenging things a person can do. It helps us focus our energies to reach the next level.

Ask and answer: "As a person, I believe…" This illuminates your values. Most people's stated values don't match their actions — and their actions become unguided reactions to life.

My primary rule: you must be willing to help yourself MORE than you expect me to help you.

"What you THINK determines how you FEEL determines what you DO. Head · Heart · Hands." — Jim Kwik

As a person, I believe:

  • Work every day to pursue your full potential
  • Grow every day in MIND and BODY
  • Obstacles and challenges are blessings that make you stronger
  • All things happen for a reason
  • Your present is the result of past decisions
  • Be disciplined in living an intentional life
  • If you do something, do it as best you can — or don't do it
